The purpose of the project is to help the Anime fan/viewers to get a recommendations for Animes. I decided to work on this project because being a big fan of Anime myslef, at times I would want to look for anime that are similar to certain Animes and had to google it and ask other people. I just thought it was a complicated process. So, I just decided to make an app myself that will help to solve that issue. Besides getting the recommendations for Animes, users can also search for Animes, and view trending and upcoming Animes like they can in other sites.
